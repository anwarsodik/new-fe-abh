Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a reservation?
You can easily make a reservation by searching for your desired dates and location on our homepage, selecting an apartment, and following the seamless checkout process.
What is your cancellation policy?
Our cancellation policy varies depending on the property and booking length. Most properties offer free cancellation up to 48 hours before check-in. Please review the specific terms on the property page.
Are utilities included in monthly rentals?
Yes, for our monthly premium apartments, standard utilities such as water, high-speed WiFi, and general maintenance are included in your rent. Electricity allowances differ by unit.
